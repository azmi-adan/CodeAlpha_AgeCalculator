About Chronos

What is Chronos?

Chronos is a premium age calculator that helps you determine your exact age in years, months, and days. It also provides additional information like your next birthday, birth day of the week, and total days lived.

Features

  • Precise age calculation
  • Beautiful, responsive design
  • Multiple theme options
  • Customizable settings
  • Birthday celebration
  • Share your results

Technologies

Built with HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks or libraries required.
